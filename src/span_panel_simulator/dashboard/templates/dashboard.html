Getting started

  1. Click a simulator configuration to view it. Templates are read-only. A running simulator appears as a discovered panel in the SpanPanel integration (default configs excluded).
  2. Clone creates an editable copy from a template or from your real panel — cloning your panel preserves recorder history per circuit.
  3. Model opens the what-if view; add battery, PV, or circuits and compare before/after. Edits mark equipment as SYN; click the badge to revert to REC.
  4. Purge removes recorder history written by the simulated panel's sensors if you added the simulated panel to Home Assistant's integration.
